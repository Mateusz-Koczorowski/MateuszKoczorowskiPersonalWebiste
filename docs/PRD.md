# Product Requirements Document

## Purpose

Create a focused technical brand website for a Senior QA Engineer. The site should communicate judgment, engineering depth, quality leadership, and practical experience without relying on inflated metrics or confidential client details.

## Audience

- Engineering leaders evaluating senior QA or quality engineering expertise
- Product and delivery teams looking for quality strategy support
- QA engineers interested in automation, AI-assisted workflows, and technical growth
- Conference organizers and community peers

## Initial Scope

- Repository setup and production-ready architecture
- Landing page foundation with the primary brand positioning
- Reusable component conventions
- Documentation for brand, UX, architecture, AI usage, backlog, and stories

## Deferred Scope

- Full case study pages
- Speaking archive
- Article system
- Contact workflow
- Analytics integration
- CMS or MDX content pipeline

## Content Principles

- Be concrete, but do not invent numbers.
- Describe public patterns, not confidential projects.
- Explain trade-offs and decisions.
- Prefer concise technical language over marketing claims.
- Clearly separate published work from planned content.

## Success Criteria

- The repository can be installed, linted, typechecked, and built.
- The first screen clearly signals senior QA engineering.
- The design foundation supports a dark, premium, minimal SaaS aesthetic.
- Documentation gives future implementation work a clear direction.
