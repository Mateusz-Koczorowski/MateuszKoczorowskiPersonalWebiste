# Branding

## Positioning

A senior quality engineering voice focused on practical automation, quality strategy, risk-aware delivery, and responsible AI-assisted QA workflows.

## Brand Attributes

- Technical
- Calm
- Precise
- Strategic
- Evidence-led
- Human-reviewed

## Voice

Use confident, plain engineering language. Avoid hype, vague excellence claims, and exaggerated outcomes. The tone should feel senior, direct, and useful.

## Visual Direction

- Dark technical premium
- Minimal SaaS interface patterns
- Strong typography with restrained density
- Subtle motion, never decorative overload
- High contrast and clear focus states
- Accent colors used to guide attention, not dominate the page

## Content Guardrails

- No lorem ipsum
- No fake metrics
- No confidential project names
- No client-identifying details without explicit permission
- No claims that cannot be supported in public
